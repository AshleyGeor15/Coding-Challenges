How to Use Your Llama: School Edition


This topic comes from one of my young readers, Emily, she wants to know how she can convince her mom to let take her llama, Fredick Fluffington, to school with her. Without a doubt, Emily has come to the right place to find the solution to her problem. So here are some tips to help out with your Llama drama.

  1. Save your feet, and ride a Llama!
    • Llamas have the ability to travel for hundreds of miles, and withstand harsh enviromental conditions.

  2. Hype up the party, Llama!
    • Llamas are very social animals, so they are always in groups called herds.
  3. Need a Study buddy? No prob-llama!
    • Llamas are smart and easy to train. So, if you need help with basic math to multi-variable calculus. Call your local llama for some assistance!
